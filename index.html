CATAN

Catan is one the world's most popular board games. It is available worlwide in over 40 language. To date, over 40 million games have been sold.


Since its inception, it has gained a loyal following across the world.


Parts of its success is attributed to its well-designed gameplay where numerous strategies are created and evolve allowing players to keep coming back for more!


Catan Overview:


  • Catan is a board game for two-four players.
  • The players compete with one another to gather resources and build the biggest settlement.
  • It takes approximately 1hr to play.
